About CCA

Continuous Clearing Auction (CCA) is Uniswap's fair token launch mechanism. All successful bidders pay the same final clearing price, eliminating front-running and timing games.

  • Fair Price Discovery: Uniform clearing price for all participants
  • Time-weighted Supply: Tokens released gradually over auction period
  • Graduation Mechanism: Requires minimum raise to complete
